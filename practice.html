Take a look at some of the best episodes of 'Friends', the story of six friends living in Manhattan, who experience comfort and companionship with each other as they indulge in many adventures.

  • The show was originally called insomnia cafe
  • Ross and Rachel weren't the key relationship. Instead, Joey and Monica were supposed to be love interests.
  • The opening credits were shot at Warner Bros. studio, not in New York.
  • The cast went on a Vegas trip before the show aired.
  • Lisa Kudrow hated playing guitar at first.
  • Many people, including Lisa Kudrow, thought that Chandler was gay.
